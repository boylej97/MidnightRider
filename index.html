About Me

The Rider
Behind the
Helmet

Born and raised in Scotland, I've been tearing up roads since I could twist a throttle. From the sweeping highland passes to the tight coastal bends — every mile tells a story.

I share those stories on TikTok and Instagram. Ride footage, gear reviews, Scottish scenery you won't believe, and the raw reality of life on two wheels in one of the world's most dramatic landscapes.

No filter. No script. Just the road, the machine, and the moment.
